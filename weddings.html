Gallery / 03Weddings & Bridal

The vow,
and what surrounds it.

From the small civil ceremony to the multi-day cultural celebration. Mehendi, nikah, baraat, reception, the morning after. Each event approached with patience and a documentary eye.

I.Approach

“I shoot weddings the way I shoot a quiet morning. Slowly, carefully, with one foot in the room and one foot out of it.”

II.A wedding day, frame by frame

What I cover.

i

Getting ready

The dress, the dupatta, the small letters and longer hugs.

ii

The mehendi

Color, sound, food. Always shot mostly handheld.

iii

Nikah / ceremony

Quiet, restrained, careful. Every signature, every blessing, every silent moment.

iv

Portraits

20-40 minutes alone with the couple. The rest of the day will not give us this.

v

Reception

The toasts, the dance floor, the table the couple never gets to sit at.
