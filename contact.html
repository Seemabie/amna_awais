IV.Quick answers

Frequently asked.

01

How far in advance should I book?

+

For weddings, six to twelve months. For newborn sessions, while you are still pregnant. For everything else, four to six weeks is usually enough.

02

What is your turnaround time?

+

A small sneak peek within 48 hours. The full edited gallery in two to three weeks. Weddings in four to six.

03

Do you travel?

+

Yes. Bay Area travel within three hours is included on all sessions. Beyond that, I quote travel as part of the package. For destination weddings, I have shot in Karachi, Antalya, Istanbul, and Princess Islands.

04

Do I get the raw files?

+

No. Edited high-resolution files only. Raw files are part of my craft, the way a chef would not hand over a half-cooked plate.

05

What if my newborn arrives early?

+

Your due date is a placeholder. Once baby arrives, you let me know and we book the session within ten days of the actual birth. I keep newborn slots flexible.

06

Can you photograph in our home?

+

Almost always yes, and I usually prefer it. Most of my favourite frames live in the rooms my clients already love.

07

Do you accept payment plans?

+

Yes. A 30% retainer holds the date. The remaining balance can be split across two or three installments through HoneyBook or direct transfer.
